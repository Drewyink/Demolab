New Accumulator

Cross-accumulation allows spending in one bucket to count toward another. Common in combined medical+Rx plans and dual-eligible scenarios.

Cross-Accumulation Scenarios
• Combined Medical+Rx: All spend (medical + pharmacy) flows into one shared deductible bucket
• Medical-first: Medical claims draw down shared deductible; Rx only after medical threshold
• Dual OOP: Each service type has its own OOP but both count toward combined family max
• HDHP HSA: All HDHP-qualified expenses must meet combined deductible before any cost-sharing
