Account Type Comparison
• HSA: Member-owned, portable, triple tax advantage, investable, HDHP required, no use-or-lose
• HRA: Employer-owned, employer funds only, not portable, employer defines eligible expenses
• Health FSA: Use-or-lose (w/ carryover up to $660), pre-funded, full election available Jan 1
• LPFSA: Limited to dental/vision, compatible with HSA
• DCFSA: Dependent care, $5K/HH limit, not for medical expenses
