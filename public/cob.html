Method Reference
• Standard: Secondary pays up to its own allowed minus what primary paid. Most common.
• Non-Dup: Secondary only pays if its benefit > primary payment. Reduces secondary payer savings.
• MOB: Secondary applies its normal benefit to the claim, reduces by primary payment.
• Carve-Out: Secondary benefit is reduced dollar-for-dollar by what primary paid.
