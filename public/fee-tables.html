RBRVS Formula
Allowed Amount = (Work RVU + Practice Expense RVU + Malpractice RVU) × GPCI × Conversion Factor
Example: 3.5 total RVU × 1.0 GPCI × 36.04 CF = $126.14 allowed
Your Contract Rate: Allowed × 110% (Preferred) = $138.75 paid to provider
