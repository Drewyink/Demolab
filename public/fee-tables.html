RBRVS Formula
Allowed Amount = (Work RVU + PE RVU + Malpractice RVU) x GPCI x Conversion Factor
Example: 3.5 RVU x 1.0 GPCI x 36.04 CF = $126.14 allowed
Contract: Allowed x 110% (Preferred) = $138.75 paid to provider
